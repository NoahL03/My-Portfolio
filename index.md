# Noah Long

Noah Long is a future technical writer with an interdisciplinary background, including visual communication, UX/UI design, and digital media. His primary interest, web-based documentation, allows him to combine technical writing with visual design principles to optimize how content is structured and presented for usability. In his current internship, he is learning more about technical writing from an industry veteran while gaining hands-on experience analyzing, creating, editing, and revising user manuals for several consumer brands. This portfolio reflects his interdisciplinary approach by presenting work in both web-based and traditional formats, highlighting how content can be adapted for different user needs.
